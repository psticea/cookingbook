title
Marry Me Chicken Pasta

prepTime
30

servings
6

effortLevel
medium

image
images/pasta/mary-me-chicken-pasta.jpg

ingredients
chicken breasts sliced - 800 g
medium onion minced - 1 pcs
garlic minced - 4 cloves
sundried tomatoes chopped - 100 g
chicken stock - 300 ml
cooking cream - 200 ml
parmesan cheese grated - 60 g
salt - 1/2 tsp
ground black pepper - 1/2 tsp
vegetable oil or sundried tomatoes oil - 2 Tbsp (30 ml)
unsalted butter - 2 Tbsp (28 g)
chili flakes - 1 tsp
oregano - 1/2 tsp
thyme - 1/2 tsp
fresh basil leaves - 1 tsp
pasta to boil for serving (optional) - 600 g

instructions
Season chicken with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat the oil and brown the chicken for 4-5 minutes on each side or until golden brown and cooked through. Transfer onto a plate.
Add the butter to the skillet and cook the onions for 5 min. Add the minced garlic and saute for a minute or until it's fragrant.
Add the chicken stock, and deglaze the pan, scraping any bits stuck to the bottom of the pan with a wooden spoon.
Adjust the heat to medium-low, and add the heavy cream and the parmesan cheese. Allow the sauce to simmer for a couple of minutes, then season with chili flakes, thyme, and oregano.
Season with salt and pepper to your taste, add the sundried tomatoes then put the chicken back in the sauce and let the sauce simmer and thicken for a few more minutes.
Garnish with chopped fresh basil leaves and serve warm over pasta or rice or mashed potatoes.
