title
Potato Soup

prepTime
30

servings
6

effortLevel
easy

image
images/soups-and-stews/potato-soup.jpg

ingredients
bacon or cabanos cubes - 200 g
grated carrots - 200 g
fine chopped yellow onion - 300 g
potatoes cubed at 1 cm - 800 g
salt (or vegeta containing 15g salt) - 15 g
finely chopped parsley - 10 g
finely chopped dill - 10 g
vegetable oil - 3 Tbsp
egg yolks - 2 pcs
sour cream - 4 Tbsp
water - 2 liters

instructions
In a saucepan, bring to medium heat 1 Tbsp of oil, add the bacon and cook for 2 minutes.
Add the remaining vegetable oil and when hot add the onions and carrots. Cook for about 3 minutes until they start softening.
Add 2 liters of water and heat the mix to a boil, then add the salt.
Add the cubed potatoes and boil for about 15 minutes, until the potatoes are well cooked.
Cut the heat and once the boiling stops add the mix of yolks and sour cream. Add the chopped dill and parsley. Taste and add more salt if needed.
