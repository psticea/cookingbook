title
Smashed Burger

prepTime
20

servings
2

effortLevel
easy

image
images/burgers-and-wraps/smashed-beef-burger.jpg

ingredients
ground beef - 200 g
onion - 1 pcs
burger buns - 4 pcs
broccoli - 1 pcs
mayo - 2 Tbsp
mustard - 1 Tbsp
chopped pickles - 1 Tbsp
paprika - 1 pinch
pepper - 10-15 cranks
cheese of your choice - 2 slices
sliced tomato to serve - 2 slices
salt - 1 pinch

instructions
Preheat the saute pan by setting it on medium heat.
Grab 100g of beef meat and shape into two balls. Cut up the onion into slices.
Place the burgers in the pan and smash down with a spatula or greased pan. Top with onion and season with salt.
Cook for 1 min then flip to cook the other side. In the meantime, roughly chop the broccoli.
Then place a slice of cheese right on top and let it melt. Remove once the burger is cooked, followed by the buns to steam. Remove once the buns are steamed.
Then drizzle in some oil, add in the chopped broccoli along with some water and a pinch of salt. Cover to steam.
For the burger sauce, combine the mayo, mustard, pickles, paprika, and pepper. Mix to combine.
Spread the burger sauce on the bun and place a tomato down. Close up the burger and serve with broccoli!
