Tuna Phili Spread

prepTime
10

servings
4

effortLevel
easy

image
images/salads-and-bites/tuna-phili-spread.jpg

ingredients
philadelphia cheese - 300 g
sour cream - 2 Tbsp
can of tuna chunks - 200 g
lemon juice - 2 Tbsp
chopped parsley - 2 Tbsp
green onions thinly sliced - 4 pcs
salt - to taste

instructions
Drain the tuna chunks before adding them.
Add all the ingredients, excluding the green onion to a blender and mix everything.
Garnish with green onions. Add more salt if needed.
Serve with bread.
