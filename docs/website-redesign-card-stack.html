Pasta

Marry Me Chicken Pasta

⏱️ 35 min
👥 4
💰 2.40/p
🧾 9.60
4 servings
4
  1. Season and sear chicken until golden brown on both sides.
  2. Build the cream sauce with garlic, sun-dried tomatoes, and parmesan.
  3. Cook pasta al dente, reserving 1 cup of pasta water.
  4. Combine everything, loosen with pasta water, garnish with basil.

Personal Notes

A weeknight hero — the sauce is the star. Reserve pasta water to loosen it.

Guide

Cooking Basics

A handful of rules that will make every recipe easier and tastier.

📖

Read the recipe completely

Before you start cooking, read it through. No mid-cook surprises.

🥕

Prep all ingredients in advance

Mise en place. Having everything at hand makes cooking relaxed and fun.

🧂

Don't skip the seasoning

It's a tiny cost that makes a big difference in the final result.

🔪

Use a well-sharpened knife

Any knife should be sharpened every few weeks. Safer, faster, better.
